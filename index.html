HOW TO GROW AN AVOCADO PLANT


For this method you'll need:
An avocado seed, Cocktail sticks, Jar or Glass, 10-inch Pot, Potting soil & Trowel


image source/PieceOfARainbow

Follow the steps below to grow your own Avocado Plant!

  1. Save an avocado pit (without cutting into or breaking it) and wash off any residue. Let it dry, then insert 3-4 cocktail sticks about halfway up the side of the pit.

  2. Suspend the broad end of the pit down in a drinking glass or jar. Fill it with enough water to submerge the bottom third of the seed.

  3. Place the glass in a warm spot out of direct sunlight and change the water regularly. Roots and a sprout should appear in about 2-6 weeks. If not, start with another seed.

  4. When the sprout gets to be about 6 inches tall, cut it back to about 3 inches to encourage more root growth.

  5. Once the stem grows out again, plant the pit in a 10-inch pot filled with rich potting soil. Now it's time to let your avocado tree grow, grow, grow!
